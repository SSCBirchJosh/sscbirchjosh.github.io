About Me

Hello There!

Welcome to the online portfolio of me, Joshua Birch! Here you will find information about me, my skills, my work and my career as I adventure through the rough terrain that is Computer Science! Further down you will find past & upcoming projects with the addition of skills that are essential to the world of Computer Science.

So, why are you here? Well, I suppose its to find out at least one thing about me. It's your lucky day dear viewer, as I will tell you everything about me, in a professional manner of course. Well, firstly I am a computer science student at South Staffordshire College, studying at Level 3. I am extending my education by attending University, Staffordshire University to be precise, where I will be completing a degree in Creative Computing Science. I hope to further this even more by completing a masters in Game Engineering, as gaming is my passion. Oh yeah, I forgot to say, I'm a nerd! Games are my life and I, one day, dream of developing in a highly regarded studio. For now, I am still in the grinding process of, one, proving myself to the world and two, getting the qualifications needed in order to be able to live my dream. So! join me through my career in Computer Science...
